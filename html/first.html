Types of Triangles

Equilateral Triangle
An equilateral triangle has all three sides of equal length. As a result, all of its angles are also equal, each measuring 60Â°. This type of triangle is highly symmetrical.
Isosceles Triangle
An isosceles triangle has two sides of equal length, and the angles opposite these equal sides are also equal. The third side has a different length, making the two remaining angles distinct from the equal ones.
Scalene Triangle
A scalene triangle has all three sides of different lengths. Consequently, all of its angles are also of different measures, making it the least symmetrical type of triangle.
